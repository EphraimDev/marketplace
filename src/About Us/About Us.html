About Us

The International Therapist Directory is an online listing of professional mental health therapist familiar with the Third Culture Kid and international expatriate experiences.

With over 250 members, in more than 40 different countries, this resource list therapist, counselors, psychologist, and psychiatrist interested in providing culturally sensitive cross-cultural treatment and care for today's international expats community.

Expats looking for a local English-speaking therapist, a marriage/relationship counselor sensitivie to cross-cultural dynamics, or a psychotherapist attuned to the nuances of a TCK upbringing will find in this site the most comprehensive directory of it's kind.
